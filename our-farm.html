Inside The Farm

A closer look at our 20,000-bird layer facility in Rajkot. We believe that exceptional products are the direct result of a meticulous environment, superior infrastructure, and uncompromising nutrition.

Premium Feed for Premium Quality

At Urban Eggs, we know that a world-class egg begins long before it is laid—it begins with what our birds eat. We utilize highly specialized, nutrition-dense feed formulations to ensure our flock receives exactly what they need to thrive.

Our feed is carefully balanced with essential vitamins, minerals, and high-quality proteins. By utilizing precise calcium levels, we guarantee strong, resilient shells, while a tailored mix of grains produces a rich, healthy yolk.

Furthermore, quality control is woven into our daily operations. Through rigorous digital data tracking, we monitor daily feed inputs down to the exact metric, ensuring absolute consistency across our entire 20,000-bird production line. Better feed simply means a better, safer, and tastier egg for your table.

Engineered for Avian Health

Our infrastructure is designed entirely around the well-being of our flock. Operating on a robust California A-type open-air cage system, our sheds prioritize natural sunlight and continuous airflow.

Because we do not rely on closed, climate-controlled environments, our sheds are engineered to harness optimal natural ventilation. This constant supply of fresh air dramatically reduces stress and maximizes flock health and comfort, mimicking a more natural environment while maintaining the strict hygiene benefits of a modern industrial setup.

From our secure electrical installations right down to daily depletion tracking, every inch of the farm operates with efficiency, safety, and hygiene as our highest priorities.
